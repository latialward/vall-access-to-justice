Law Librarians created the *Guide to Virginia Legal Information Resources* to assist researchers in finding self-help legal information and to aid librarians in reference work and developing their collections of self-help legal resources. Additional information about access to justice and the creation of the *Guide to Virginia Legal Information Resources* is available at the blog posts entitled [Self-Help Legal Information Resources in Virginia](https://lispsr.wordpress.com/2023/11/01/self-help-legal-information-resources-in-virginia/) and [Self-Help Legal Information and Access to Justice](https://informationandlaw.com/2025/04/29/self-help-legal-information-and-access-to-justice/).
